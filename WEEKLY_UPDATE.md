## 🚀 Headline: "In Process" roadmaps

A brand-new way for builders to share the story of a project over time — eras, milestones, and life chapters, with a native process log. This was the week's biggest lift (PRs #144–#148).

- **Roadmaps with eras, milestones & life chapters**, synced to `inprocess.world`
- **Write to In Process** — a connect flow that mints milestones as "moments"
- **Process-log post types** — moment / thought / link / embed
- **Real image upload** across the whole In Process editor
- **Precision dates** — pick exact day, month, or year for eras and milestones (no more vague text labels)
- **Project-scoped roadmaps** with inline editing on both world and project pages
- **Project-first creation flow** plus a plain-language explainer so it's clear what In Process is for
- **Redesigned surface** — branded masthead, selectable milestones, filtered log, and a one-project-at-a-time roadmap switcher on the In Process tab
- **Milestone detail view** — tapping a milestone opens a detail modal; editing is now an explicit, separate step
